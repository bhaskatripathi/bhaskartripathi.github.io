About me

Glad that you could join me here! As a computer hardware enthusiast, I watch a lot of keynotes of different companies and watch them slap the "AI" tag with all their products. With AI, they were able to unlock a lot of functionalities and this got me excited and I started learning about it.

Currently, I am working as Machine learning intern at Salesken on conversational AI. Here, I am working on Hyperparameter optimization of reinforcement learning agents and language understanding tasks. Also, I am actively contributing to the library FinRL

I am interested in how AI is going to shape the products of the future and my interests lies in:

  • Quantitative trading
  • End-to-End Hyperparameter tuning & Ensemble Learning
  • Natural Language processing in a financial setting

Previously, I worked on areas of Convolutional Neural Networks, data-driven thermal analysis and anomaly detection.
